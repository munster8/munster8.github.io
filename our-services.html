Our Services

Navigating the Malaysia My Second Home (MM2H) application process can be complex, but our agency is here to make it simple and stress-free. As an authorized consultancy, we provide end-to-end support to foreign nationals interested in making Malaysia their second home. Our experienced team ensures that each application meets the program's standards, increasing the likelihood of approval and providing guidance at every step.

Why Choose Us?

With years of experience and a deep understanding of the MM2H program, we provide reliable and transparent consultancy services tailored to your needs. From initial consultation to full relocation support, our goal is to ensure a smooth, stress-free experience, helping you make Malaysia your second home with confidence.

Key Services

  • Eligibility Assessment
    We start with a comprehensive assessment of your profile to determine your eligibility for the MM2H program. Our consultants will review your financial standing, family requirements, and other relevant factors to help you select the most suitable tier for your application.

  • Document Preparation & Submission
    Our team assists with the preparation and organization of all necessary documents, including financial statements, proof of identity, and other supporting materials. We handle the submission process on your behalf, ensuring that all forms are correctly completed and promptly submitted.

  • Financial Planning & Advisory
    Each MM2H tier has unique financial requirements. We offer expert advice on how to meet these requirements and provide guidance on banking and investment options in Malaysia, ensuring that you meet the financial criteria smoothly.

  • Application Follow-up & Status Updates
    Once your application is submitted, we stay in close contact with the relevant authorities to monitor progress. We provide regular updates on the status of your application and address any questions or issues that may arise during the review process.

  • Visa Processing & Extension Support
    After approval, we assist with the visa issuance process, ensuring you receive your 5-year, renewable pass. When it comes time to renew, our team will guide you through the extension process, helping you maintain compliance with MM2H requirements.

  • Family & Dependent Assistance
    If you are bringing family members under the program, we will guide you through the specific requirements and documentation for spouses and dependents, making the process seamless for your entire family.

  • Relocation & Settlement Support
    Moving to a new country can be overwhelming. Our services include advice on finding suitable housing, understanding local regulations, and accessing essential services to help you settle into life in Malaysia.
